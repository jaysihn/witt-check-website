Privacy Policy for Witt Check

Effective date: June 29, 2025

Witt Check ("the app") is developed by morrDigitalCo. This privacy policy explains how Witt Check collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information We Collect

Witt Check may access the following on your device:

  • Account Information: Display name, email, and password for account creation and login.
  • Training Data: Your practice history, personalized jabs, and feedback are stored locally on your device. We do not store or sync this data externally.
  • Firebase Authentication: Used for optional account login and subscription access control.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to enable features in the app, including:

  • Generating personalized jabs and feedback for your wit training
  • Saving local settings, training history, and progress for your convenience
  • Managing premium feature access for subscribers

We do not collect personal data, sell it, or share it with advertisers.

Third-Party Services

Witt Check uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – for secure user login
  • RevenueCat – for managing subscriptions and payments
  • Google Play – for app distribution and billing

Payments and Subscriptions

The app is free to download on the Google Play Store.

Optional premium features are available via in-app subscription, handled securely through RevenueCat and Google Play Billing. We do not store any payment details ourselves.

Children’s Privacy

Witt Check is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
